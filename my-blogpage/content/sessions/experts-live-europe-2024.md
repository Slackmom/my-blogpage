## What This Session Was About

For neurodiverse employees, virtual 3D spaces aren't just a novelty — they can be a genuine leveler. This session made the case for Microsoft Mesh as an inclusion tool, backed by real examples and practical implementation guidance.

Immersive spaces reduce some of the most challenging aspects of traditional office environments: unpredictable social cues, sensory overload, the pressure to perform neurotypical communication patterns. In a Mesh environment, everyone is an avatar. The playing field changes.

## Key Topics

- The specific challenges neurodiverse employees face in traditional and hybrid work settings
- How Microsoft Mesh changes the social dynamics of virtual meetings
- Avatar-based communication: reducing performance anxiety and enabling authentic participation
