## What This Session Was About

Minecraft for business? Seriously? Yes — seriously. This session showed exactly how and why Minecraft Education works as a team facilitation tool, not despite being a game, but because of it.

When teams play Minecraft together, the game becomes a behavioral mirror. Communication patterns, leadership dynamics, resource sharing, conflict resolution — all of it shows up in how a team navigates a Minecraft challenge. And because the stakes are low, people experiment more honestly than they do in real work settings.

## Key Topics

- Why low-stakes environments produce more honest team behavior
- How to design Minecraft challenges that reveal real collaboration patterns
- Facilitating the debrief: turning game observations into workplace insight
- What teams take away — and how it transfers back to Teams and SharePoint
- Practical setup: what you need to run a Minecraft team session
