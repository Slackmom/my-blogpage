## What This Session Was About

The biggest Copilot adoption problem isn't technical — it's emotional. People are anxious, skeptical, or just indifferent. This session used Minecraft as an entry point to reframe that relationship: from threat to tool, from confusion to curiosity.

By drawing direct parallels between Minecraft mechanics and generative AI — the logic of prompting, the nature of creative iteration, the way constraints produce better outputs — we gave audiences a mental model that actually sticks.

## Key Topics

- Why AI anxiety is the real adoption blocker
- Minecraft as a metaphor for generative AI: building blocks, iteration, emergent creativity
- Prompt engineering through a game-design lens
- Practical M365 Copilot workflows that even skeptical users can get started with
- Live demo: building prompts like building in Minecraft
