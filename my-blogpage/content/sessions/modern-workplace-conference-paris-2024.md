## What This Session Was About

Microsoft Mesh promises a new kind of collaboration — but getting from "I've seen the demo" to "we're actually using this" involves real decisions, real setup, and real change management. This session closed that gap.

A full zero-to-hero walkthrough: from enabling Mesh in your tenant, to building your first custom environment, to running a meeting your colleagues will actually talk about afterward. Live demos throughout, with honest commentary on where the friction points are.

## What We Covered

- Enabling and configuring Microsoft Mesh in a Teams tenant
- Building custom Mesh environments: tools, templates, and tips
- Designing the right experiences for the right use cases
- How to introduce Mesh to skeptical teams
- A live immersive session demo — including what goes wrong and how to recover
