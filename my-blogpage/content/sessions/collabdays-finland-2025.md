## What This Session Was About

Copilot adoption is the challenge of 2025. Most organizations have the license. Far fewer have the usage. The gap isn't technical — it's motivational, and UX design can close it.

This session built a Power App live that used game UX patterns — onboarding flows, progress indicators, contextual hints, celebration moments — to make Copilot feel approachable and rewarding instead of overwhelming and optional. The app was built during the session, with the audience directing decisions.

## What We Built

- A Copilot onboarding app with game-inspired UX patterns
- Progress tracking that motivates rather than measures
- A tips-and-tricks feed that surfaces Copilot use cases contextually
- Celebration mechanics for adoption milestones

## Key Takeaways

By the end of the session, attendees had a working prototype and a reusable framework for thinking about any adoption app through the lens of game design.
